Ember 1.6.1 Released

Posted By: Robert Jackson – Jul 15, 2014

Shortly after Ember 1.6.0 was released a regression was identified regarding custom error substates. The error handling that was added to make debugging errors thrown in the Router's promise hooks inadvertently caused the error substates to be completely ignored.
