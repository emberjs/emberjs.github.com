Ember Meetup Assets

We've created Meetup group badges and branding to help give our groups a consistent feel. You can create your own by downloading the PSD. We've got some other materials in the works and will post them here and email the organizers' Google Group as things come together.

If you're using Meetup.com to manage your group, you can also use this header template for your group's pages. It's optimized for Meetup (960 x 150); you can just add your location and you're good to go.

Logos

If you have questions about acceptable uses of the Ember logo, or other format requests, please get in touch. In general, manipulations of any sort are not allowed.

  • Logo in Illustrator format
  • Logo in PNG format, transparent background

Stickers

The Ember Core Team has a small stash of Ember stickers donated by the folks at Tilde. These are distributed as requested while in stock. If you'd like to print your own Ember stickers, here are the files you'll need for the canonical ones.

  • Ember Logo Stickers, best printed die-cut at 3.0" x 1.25"
  • Ember Mascot Stickers, best printed die-cut at 3" x 3"

We recommend printing your stickers with StickerMule, who are long time supporters of Open Source. The link here will also get you $10 off your first order.
